Let's go on a trip to Paris

Bonjour Paris!

Paris is known to be the City of Light but to some the City of Love. A vibrant and gorgeous city that enchants visitors with its deep history, iconic landmarks, and delicious food.

Best places to visit in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower
  2. Louvre Museum
  3. Canal Saint-Martin
  4. Champs-Élysées
  5. Musée de la Vie Romantique

Tips for Your Trip

  • Respect the local culture and etiquette by greeting shopkeepers and restaurant staff with a polite "bonjour," avoiding loud conversations in public spaces, and be patient when waiting in line.
  • Try the unique French cuisine by dining at local bistros, brasseries, and patisseries. Don't miss out on trying classic dishes like croissants, escargot, coq au vin, and crème brûlée.
  • Take a leisurely stroll along the Seine River for breathtaking views of the city.
  • ExploreParis's rich cultural offerings, including museums, galleries, theaters, and live performances. Attend a ballet at the Palais Garnier, explore contemporary art at the Centre Pompidou, or stroll along the Seine River at sunset.
  • Consider purchasing a Paris Pass for access to multiple attractions and skip-the-line privileges.
